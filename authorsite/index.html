AuthorsRealm is a site created by readers and writers for readers and writers. Throughout years of taking writing and intensive literary analysis courses, Angela, the mind behind Authors Realm, hasn’t found an online writing platform that functions to create a platform that intrigues readers and writers alike, and hopes to conjoin the two together. After all, a writer is a reader who just wants to read the story they’re creating. Join the journey as Angela researches and writes.

Successful Writers

J.R.R. Tolkien. Stephen King. Flannery O'Connor. Every writer has a writer’s style and voice. Have you ever wondered what makes an author intriguing and relevant? The Brave New World author Aldous Huxley said, “Words can be like X-rays if you use them properly—they’ll go through anything. You read and you’re pierced.” We explore the techniques of writers who have crafted words that became X-rays. We also discuss how to replicate techniques in our own voices.
