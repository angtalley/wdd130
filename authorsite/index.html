AuthorsRealm is a site created by readers and writers for readers and writers. The mind behind AuthorsRealm is pursuing a degree in Interdisciplinary Studies - Creative Writing at Brigham Young University Idaho. Throughout the years of taking writing and intensive literary analysis courses, Angela has incurred her list of questions and interests that she wants answers for. Throughout her years pursuing writing, Angela hasn’t been able to find an online writing platform that functions to create a platform that intrigues readers and writers alike, and hopes to conjoin the two together. After all, a writer is a reader who just wants to read the story they’re creating. Join the journey as Angela researches and even writes her own novels.
