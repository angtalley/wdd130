Book Recommendations

“You can never get a cup of tea large enough or a book long enough to suit me,” C.S. Lewis said. Well, what about five books, Lewis? Have you ever been caught up in a novel, or world, and when it finishes, you’re left wondering what is next? Fear no longer! This page explores writers of similar genres or story devices so you know what to read next. You’ll never be left bored or in a readers lag following this page. This page is created by readers, for readers. Leave a comment for a novel we’ve yet to discuss to find out what paths your current reading trend will take you.
