Book Recommendations

“You can never get a cup of tea large enough or a book long enough to suit me,” C.S. Lewis said. Well, what about five books, Lewis? Here, you can find writers of similar genre or story so you know what to read next. You’ll never be left bored or in a readers lag following this page. Leave a comment for a novel we’ve yet to discuss to find out what paths your current reading trend will take you.

1 / 5
milk and honey

This book is a poetry book by Rupi Kaur. It uses many real life experiences! Good read for a young adult.

2 / 5
The Complete Poetry

Despite being much older, Maya Angelou's poetry still speaks to readers today. Her use of imagery is crazy!

3 / 5
the princess saves herself in this one

Amanda Lovelace's poetry book is an interesting take on common everyday life. This book is perfect for young minds!

4 / 5
the sun and her flowers

The Canadian hit it again with this one! Rupi Kaur has a special way with words, and if you are in the market for changing your mind about the way you see the world, then Kaur's writing is a must!

4 / 5
the things I didn't say in therapy

You will feel like you're sitting on a leather couch with Logan Duane. This book brings imagery and fluttery words together!
